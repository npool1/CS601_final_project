The crowded kitchen is a space to share food stories and recipes. The site is produced and developed by Lost Otter Co-founder Adrienne Ostberg, and focuses on the culinary exploits of her and her husband, Nick. The hope is to bring you into the couple's small home kitchen to explore their love and knowledge of food. Please explore and feel to share your own recipes and stories!
